WHAT IS THE DUBAI GOLDEN VISA?

The Dubai Golden Visa was introduced with the intention of motivating and retaining “talented people and great minds” in the United Arab Emirates. This program will help provide opportunities and drive the UAE economy to greater heights. As of now, over 45,000 individuals have received the prestigious UAE Golden Visa.

Investors, entrepreneurs, talented professionals and students are eligible to receive the Golden Visa in Dubai and will be approved to do so by the government agencies of their respective fields. Obtaining the Dubai Golden Visa has certain requirements and can depend on certain factors. This includes the type of golden visa application, the kind of golden visa services you require, duration etc.

GOLDEN VISA ELIGIBILITY

It is important to know whether you are eligible to get a golden visa in Dubai. However, it is only issued to certain categories of individuals.

Entrepreneurs

The UAE government issued Golden Visas to entrepreneurs who want to set up their business in Dubai or want to expand their pre-existing business in the UAE.

Investors

Foreign investors who want to start their commercial operations in the UAE, make real estate investment or invest in UAE banks are eligible to apply for the Dubai golden visa.

Exceptional Talents

Individuals who exhibit outstanding talents in certain fields are eligible for a golden visa in Dubai. This includes researchers, artists & creators, scientists, doctors, etc., who are experts in their respective domains.

Academically Gifted Students

Promising students who have consistently performed well academically and show significant promise can also apply for the UAE golden visa.

DUBAI GOLDEN VISA: REQUIREMENTS

Shuraa offers golden visa services depending on the type of visa you wish to acquire. Based on the category of eligibility and the duration of the long-term residency, applicants will have to fulfill certain requirements:

Dubai 10-year Golden Visa Requirements:

Investors

  • Minimum AED 2 million investment in trade license or UAE real estate
  • Property ownership of at least AED 2 million
  • Bank investments of at least AED 2 million for 2 years

Entrepreneurs

  • Own a business in Dubai that generate a minimum annual profit margin of AED 2 million

Directors/CEOs/Executives

  • Recipients of minimum monthly salary of AED 30,000 (must show last 6 months' pay slip)

Highly Qualified Individuals

To meet eligibility requirements for this category, applicants must fulfil at least one of the following conditions:

  • Recipients of note-worthy awards in their field
  • Recognition from the UAE or home country ministries in the form of awards or certificates
  • Appreciation letter from home country government or certification/accreditation from respective UAE ministry
  • Hold patent for invention under your name

Doctors/Researchers/Specialists

To meet eligibility requirements for this category, applicants must fulfil at least two of the following conditions:

  • Have at lease 10 years of experience in their respective fields
  • Recipients of note-worthy awards in their respective fields
  • Publication of research in any recognised journal
  • Hold patent for an invention

Gifted Students

  • Are under the age of 25 years
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 3.8
  • Hold a bachelor's degree from a university of note
